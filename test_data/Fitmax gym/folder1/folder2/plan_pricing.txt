Membership Plans and Pricing
At FitMax Gym, we offer three membership tiers tailored to meet your needs: Basic, Premium, and Family.

Basic Membership: Includes unlimited access to gym equipment during operating hours. Perfect for individuals who want a no-frills fitness experience. $20/month.
Premium Membership: Includes access to gym equipment, all group classes, sauna facilities, and one personal training session per month. $50/month.
Family Membership: Designed for families of up to 4 members. Includes Premium Membership benefits for all family members. $120/month.
We also offer discounts for students and seniors (10% off). Memberships are contract-free, with no hidden fees. Join us today to take the first step toward your fitness goals!
