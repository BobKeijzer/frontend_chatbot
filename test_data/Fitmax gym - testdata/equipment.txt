Equipment Overview
Our gym is equipped with state-of-the-art machines and tools to suit all workout styles.

Cardio Machines: Treadmills, ellipticals, rowing machines, and stair climbers, all equipped with heart-rate monitors and touchscreens for tracking progress.
Strength Training: Dumbbells ranging from 2 lbs to 100 lbs, squat racks, benches, and Smith machines.
Functional Training: Kettlebells, resistance bands, medicine balls, and a TRX suspension system for dynamic workouts.
Specialized Equipment: Plate-loaded machines for leg presses, lat pulldowns, and chest presses.
We ensure regular maintenance and cleaning of all equipment to guarantee a safe and seamless workout experience for everyone.
